🎬 Short Script – “Mama’s Heart”

Theme: Single mother, longing, self-worth
Duration: ~50 seconds

Scene 1 – Mirror Reflection

Time: 0–3s
Shot: Close-up vertical
Visual: East African woman, mid-30s, looking at herself in the bathroom mirror. Slightly tired eyes.
VO:
"Whose heart will I find tonight?"

Scene 2 – Preparing for Evening

Time: 3–8s
Shot: Medium shot
Visual: She adjusts her clothes, combs her hair, applies light makeup, anxious and hesitant.
VO:
"Will someone love me the way I am?"

Scene 3 – Phone Call / Rejection

Time: 8–15s
Shot: Close-up on her phone lighting up, she hesitates to answer.
VO:
"Every time I try…
I’m left alone again."

Scene 4 – Family Reminder

Time: 15–22s
Shot: Over-the-shoulder of her child playing in background.
Visual: She smiles faintly, hiding sadness.
VO:
"I must be strong… for my child."

Scene 5 – Emotional Vulnerability

Time: 22–30s
Shot: Medium shot, sitting on the bed, looking down at hands.
VO:
"I want to be loved… truly loved…
without masks, without fear."

Scene 6 – Glimpse of Hope

Time: 30–38s
Shot: Soft focus, she looks out a window at sunset, light on her face.
VO:
"Maybe one day someone will see me…
and love me as I am."

Scene 7 – Closing Line / Call to Emotion

Time: 38–48s
Shot: Eye-level close-up, slight tear but hopeful expression.
VO:
"I wish someone could love me… the way I am."

Scene 8 – Fade Out

Time: 48–50s
Visual: Fade to black, text overlay:
"Every mother deserves love."
Optional hashtag: #ShortFilm #SingleMom #Love
