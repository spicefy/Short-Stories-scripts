# 🎬 Short Film Shot & Action Script – “Mama’s Heart”

---

### **Scene 1 – Mirror Reflection**

**Time:** 0–3s
**Camera:** Tight close-up, vertical frame, eye-level. Slight handheld for intimacy.
**Action:** Mother stares at herself in the bathroom mirror, running fingers along the edge, faint reflection in mirror slightly distorted.
**Lighting:** Soft natural window light, half of face shadowed.
**VO / Internal Thought:**
*"Whose heart will I find tonight?"*

---

### **Scene 2 – Preparing for Evening**

**Time:** 3–8s
**Camera:** Medium shot, vertical, slow pan from torso to face.
**Action:** She adjusts her blouse, combs hair, checks herself briefly, hesitating, anxious. Slight fidgeting of hands.
**Lighting:** Warm, natural indoor light.
**VO:**
*"Will someone love me the way I am?"*

---

### **Scene 3 – Phone Call / Rejection**

**Time:** 8–15s
**Camera:** Close-up on phone screen in hand, shallow focus on her face blurred in background.
**Action:** Phone lights up with unknown caller, she hesitates, thumb hovering over answer button, shakes her head slowly.
**VO:**
*"Every time I try… I’m left alone again."*

---

### **Scene 4 – Family Reminder**

**Time:** 15–22s
**Camera:** Over-the-shoulder, blurred child in foreground, mother slightly off-center.
**Action:** She watches her child playing, faint smile but eyes sad. One hand on chest.
**VO:**
*"I must be strong… for my child."*

---

### **Scene 5 – Emotional Vulnerability**

**Time:** 22–30s
**Camera:** Medium shot, vertical, eye-level. Slightly shaky handheld for vulnerability.
**Action:** Sitting on the bed, hands clasped, looking down at her lap, deep sigh. Shoulders slump, body language heavy.
**VO:**
*"I want to be loved… truly loved… without masks, without fear."*

---

### **Scene 6 – Glimpse of Hope**

**Time:** 30–38s
**Camera:** Soft focus, vertical, over-the-shoulder or side profile facing window.
**Action:** She looks out window at sunset, light catching side of face. Slow deep breath.
**VO:**
*"Maybe one day someone will see me… and love me as I am."*

---

### **Scene 7 – Direct Address / Close-Up**

**Time:** 38–48s
**Camera:** Eye-level close-up, vertical, steady tripod.
**Action:** She looks directly into camera, one tear slowly falls. Subtle nod, lips quiver slightly.
**VO:**
*"I wish someone could love me… the way I am."*

---

### **Scene 8 – Hands Clasped**

**Time:** 48–49s
**Camera:** Extreme close-up of hands clasped on lap. Slow push-in.
**Action:** Fingers squeeze together, trembling slightly.
**Sound:** Ambient soft room tone, subtle heartbeat or sigh.

---

### **Scene 9 – Fade Out / Text Overlay**

**Time:** 49–50s
**Camera:** Fade to black, vertical frame.
**Action:** Add text overlay: *“Every mother deserves love.”*
**Sound:** Soft fade-out music or gentle chime.

---

✅ **Notes for Production:**

* Keep all shots **vertical 9:16** for Shorts/TikTok.
* Use **natural light whenever possible**, supplemented with soft warm LEDs.
* Handheld shots for emotional intimacy; tripod for close-ups where steadiness is key.
* Subtle sound design: ambient room tone, faint soft music for emotional impact.
