---

# 🎬 Title: **“The Little Shopper”**

**Length:** 45–60 seconds
**Style:** Cinematic, emotional, warm color grade
**No dialogue — only music + ambient sounds**

---

## 🎵 Background Music

Soft, uplifting instrumental (piano + light strings).
Start calm → slightly joyful → heartwarming ending.

---

# 📖 STORYLINE

A young child decides to go buy fruits alone.
He carefully counts his coins, walks to a grocery store, chooses fruits, pays proudly, and enjoys eating them — showing independence, innocence, and simple joy.

---

# 🎥 SCENE-BY-SCENE BREAKDOWN

---

## 🟢 SCENE 1 – THE DECISION (5–7 sec)

**Shot 1:**
Close-up: Kid sitting on bed/floor counting coins.
Coins make soft clinking sound.

**Shot 2:**
Insert shot: His small hand holding coins tightly.

**Shot 3:**
He looks determined.

📝 **Text Overlay:**
*"Today… I’m going shopping."*

---

## 🟢 SCENE 2 – THE WALK (7–10 sec)

**Shot 1:**
Wide shot: Kid walking down a quiet street holding coins in fist.

**Shot 2:**
Low angle shot of small feet walking.

**Shot 3:**
Sunlight hitting his face.

📝 **Text Overlay:**
*"On my own."*

---

## 🟢 SCENE 3 – THE GROCERY STORE (10–15 sec)

**Shot 1:**
Wide shot: Small grocery stall/store.

**Shot 2:**
Close-up: Kid looking at colorful fruits (bananas, apples, oranges).

**Shot 3:**
Slow motion: He gently picks one apple.

**Shot 4:**
Close-up: He counts coins on the counter.

📝 **Text Overlay:**
*"Choosing carefully."*

**Detail Idea:**
Show shopkeeper smiling (no dialogue).

---

## 🟢 SCENE 4 – THE PROUD MOMENT (7–10 sec)

**Shot 1:**
Close-up: Shopkeeper hands fruit in small paper bag.

**Shot 2:**
Kid holding bag tightly.

**Shot 3:**
Small proud smile.

📝 **Text Overlay:**
*"I did it."*

---

## 🟢 SCENE 5 – THE REWARD (10–15 sec)

**Shot 1:**
Kid sitting under a tree or outside home.

**Shot 2:**
Close-up: He washes fruit (if possible).

**Shot 3:**
Slow motion bite of fruit.

**Shot 4:**
Juice dripping slightly, happy expression.

📝 **Text Overlay:**
*"The sweetest victory."*

---

## 🟢 FINAL SHOT (5 sec)

Wide shot: Kid walking home happily eating fruit.

📝 **Final Text:**
*"Little steps. Big independence."*

Optional CTA:
*"Every small win matters."*

---

# 🎥 CAMERA STYLE GUIDE

* Shoot at 24fps for cinematic look
* Use natural light (golden hour if possible)
* Use shallow depth of field for emotional feel
* Slow-motion for fruit bite moment

---

# 🎨 COLOR GRADING IDEA (If using Premiere Pro)

* Slight warm temperature
* Soft contrast
* Lift shadows slightly
* Add light vignette

Mood: Warm, hopeful, nostalgic.
