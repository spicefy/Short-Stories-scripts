Title: God Bless The Women

God bless the women…

In the middle of the night
I heard her pray — so bitterly,
yet so softly.

Not for herself.
Never for herself.

She prayed for her children.
She prayed for their education.
She prayed for doors to open
where walls had been built.

Then she prayed…
for the man
who left her with her children.

While the world sleeps,
she carries tomorrow on her shoulders.

We praise heroes every day —
names in headlines, faces on screens —
but there are heroes we forget to praise:

The women of this world.

They don’t run from storms.
They become the shelter.

They don’t run from battles.
They stand.
They fight.
For what’s right.

God bless the women —
the silent warriors,
the midnight prayers,
the unbroken strength.

God bless the women.
